General Information

I am currently working in speech processing group in Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences. Previously, I received B.S. degree from Ocean University of China, Qingdao College, and MSc degree from Heidelberg University, Germany in 2019.
